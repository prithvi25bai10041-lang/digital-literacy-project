Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

✅ Do’s
Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts
Think before you post — consider how it reflects on you
Use strong privacy settings to protect your personal information
Share useful and positive content (achievements, learning, projects)
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation

❌ Don’ts
Don’t share personal details like address, phone number, or passwords
Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
Don’t engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
Don’t blindly trust unknown links or messages
Don’t post anything that could harm your reputation in the future
