Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

✅ Do’s
1. Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts
2. Think before you post — consider how it reflects on you
3. Use strong privacy settings to protect your personal information
4. Share useful and positive content (achievements, learning, projects)
5. Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation

❌ Don’ts
1. Don’t share personal details like address, phone number, or passwords
2. Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
3. Don’t engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
4. Don’t blindly trust unknown links or messages
5. Don’t post anything that could harm your reputation in the future
