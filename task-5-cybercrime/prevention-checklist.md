# 🛡️ Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

* Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
* Do not scan QR codes to receive money
* Always verify unknown calls claiming to be from banks or companies
* Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
* Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages
* Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history
* Install apps only from trusted sources like official app stores
* Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches

## 💰 UPI Safety Tips

* Double-check the receiver’s name before making any payment
* Do not accept collect requests from unknown users
